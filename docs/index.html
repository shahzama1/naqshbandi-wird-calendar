Google Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar on desktop.
  2. Click + next to "Other calendars" in the sidebar.
  3. Select "From URL".
  4. Paste the subscribe URL above.
  5. Click "Add calendar".

Google re-fetches the feed roughly every 12-24 hours, so new prayer times appear automatically.
